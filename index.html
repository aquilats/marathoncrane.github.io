Marathon Crane and Transport serves the Permian Basin with truck-mounted cranes and tractor trailers.
We specialize in:
  • hauling and lifting wellhead equipment in support of hydraulic fracturing
  • installation and removal of 500bbl and smaller tanks, heater treaters, and separators at tank batteries
  • hauling forklifts, compressors, coolers, vessels and other equipment with our drop-deck and flatbed trailers and semis
  • light-duty lifting at drilling rig locations for rig maintenance and repair operations.

Marathon owns and operates three truck-mounted cranes, two 15 ton and one 20 ton. The cranes with operators can be contracted for a minimum of four hours, then hourly beyond the four hour minimum. Longer term and/or recurring rates are available.

Each crane can haul a trailer to transport your equipment between your yard and the location. This adds "one-call" flexibility.

Marathon also has a tractor trailer rig with a drop-deck trailer capable of hauling equipment and portable buildings.

  • 15-ton National Crane
  • 15-ton Terex Crane
  • 20-ton National Crane
  • Tractor Trailer
Marathon Crane and Transport was recently selected by Titan Downhole Tools, LP to serve as the West Texas Yard for Titan Motors. We keep a variety of sizes and speeds on hand. We have Hot Shot Trucking partners available to quickly get the motors to the rig, or we can load our customers' preferred Hot Shots.
